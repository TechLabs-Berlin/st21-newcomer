Anmeldung

What is the process for?

Anmeldung in Berlin/Brandenburg is registering your current address with the local authorities or city registration.

What is the purpose/importance of this process?

It is important to do the registration because you need an anmeldung to open a Bank account, collect your Tax number and your Pension number etc.

What happens if you do not do this process?

It is very important to register your address within 14 days after your move in date, if you fail to register within this period, you might be fine 50€ for every month you fail to do your registration.

What are the documents needed?

To Register your address, you need the following documents: 1. “Anmeldung bei einer Meldebehörde”- It is a form you have to fill; the form contains question like your name, address, date of birth etc
2. “Wohnungsgeberbestätigung” – This is the document you get from your landlord, which confirms that you have moved in at the address, but should in case you are subletting or you are sharing a flat, the main tenant is allowed to also fill the form
3. A valid ID Card or Passport and your residence Permit, if you have one
4. Marriage Certificate (translated in German), if you are married.
How to book an appointment? You can either make a call to the Bürgeramt/Rathaus which ever one you have in the city you live, write them an Email or check their website to see if you can get an appointment.
