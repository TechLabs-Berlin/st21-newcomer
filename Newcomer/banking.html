Banking in Germany

Some official & private institutions prefer payment via German bank account (Amt, city hall, housing agencies etc).

Types of bank accounts

Girokonto, a transactional account where your expenses and earnings go into Sperrkonto / German Blocked Bank Account. Typically used to allow foreigners to acquire visas, mostly for students visa. This is to prove they have sufficient means to stay in Germany.

Online Banking

-N26 : No additional fees to make an account Free credit card Unlimited free withdrawals / 1.7% withdrawal fee worldwide Free online banking and free international money transfer Customer support available in English. You can open the account online in a few minutes with a webcam. No need Anmeldung (most cases) Easy to close the account A few minutes of video call with proof of passport or ID is sufficient Within a week after a video call, you will receive the card

-DKB: Same advantages and easy process to open the account online as N26 Don't support English customer support Free bank acount and credit card

-Revolut : Recommended for a traveler, a good free option for people who want to transfer money to another countries with no fees
